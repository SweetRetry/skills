# 国漫题材关键词库

## 仙侠 Xianxia

- **关键词**: ink wash painting fusion, flowing celestial robes, qi energy auras, cloud sea, immortal realm architecture, brush stroke textures, jade ornaments, spiritual swords, divine light beams, ethereal translucency
- **色彩**: 翠玉绿、朱砂红、金箔、水墨渐变、丝绸质感的半透明层次、月白、霜蓝
- **参考作品**: 天官赐福、魔道祖师、斗破苍穹、完美世界
- **构图特征**: 竖幅卷轴式、人物悬浮于云海、书法式动态轨迹、仙气飘渺的大留白

## 武侠 Wuxia

- **关键词**: dynamic sword choreography, flowing martial robes, bamboo forest, moonlit rooftop duel, ink splatter motion trails, weathered jianghu attire, hidden weapon details, calligraphic speed lines
- **色彩**: 水墨黑白为主、点缀赤红与暗金、月光银蓝、黄昏暖橙、旧纸质感
- **参考作品**: 一人之下、镖人、画江湖之不良人
- **构图特征**: 宽银幕电影式取景、飞檐走壁的极端透视、雨中/雪中对峙、兵器特写

## 古风 Ancient Costume

- **关键词**: traditional hanfu, palace architecture, lattice windows, silk embroidery detail, hair ornaments with jade and gold, incense smoke, scroll painting aesthetic, seasonal flora (plum blossom, lotus, chrysanthemum, bamboo)
- **色彩**: 宫廷配色——正红与明黄、青瓷蓝绿、檀木深棕、素白与墨色、刺绣的金线细节
- **参考作品**: 长歌行、风起洛阳、药屋少女
- **构图特征**: 工笔画式精致、庭院纵深、帷幔与屏风分层、四季景物衬托情绪

## 都市奇幻 Urban Fantasy

- **关键词**: modern cityscape with supernatural overlays, spirit summoning circles, glowing runes on smartphone screens, casual streetwear mixed with mystical accessories, neon talismans, phantom beasts in urban alleys
- **色彩**: 都市灰调底色 + 超自然元素的荧光色（灵力蓝、符咒金、妖气紫）、夜景霓虹
- **参考作品**: 一人之下、非人哉、灵笼
- **构图特征**: 现代建筑与灵异元素叠加、手机/耳机等现代物件与符咒并存、街景纵深

## 都市言情 Modern Romance

- **关键词**: fashionable contemporary clothing, cafe interior, city skyline, soft bokeh background, delicate facial features, expressive eyes with light reflections, wind-blown hair, seasonal atmosphere (cherry blossoms, autumn leaves, snowfall)
- **色彩**: 柔和马卡龙色系、奶茶暖调、樱花粉与薄荷绿、晨光金与黄昏紫
- **参考作品**: 怦然心动、甜美的咬痕、你好旧时光
- **构图特征**: 半身/胸像特写、浅景深虚化、对角线构图、温暖窗光或黄昏逆光

## 热血战斗 Action/Battle

- **关键词**: dynamic impact frames, shockwave rings, speed lines, debris explosion, power-up aura, torn clothing revealing battle damage, clenched fists with energy trails, dramatic low-angle hero shot
- **色彩**: 高饱和对比——烈焰红橙 vs 冷电蓝、爆炸白光、暗影浓黑
- **参考作品**: 斗罗大陆、全职高手、雄兵连
- **构图特征**: 荷兰角倾斜、极端仰视/俯视、冲击波放射线构图、碎片飞溅

## 末日废土 Post-Apocalyptic

- **关键词**: ruined cityscapes overgrown with vegetation, cracked highways, makeshift shelters from scavenged materials, gas masks and improvised armor, dust storms, abandoned vehicles half-buried in sand, faded propaganda posters peeling off walls, survivor camps with flickering firelight
- **色彩**: 沙尘黄、铁锈橙、灰绿、褪色蓝、烟雾灰、偶尔的篝火暖光作为唯一暖色
- **参考作品**: 灵笼、末日曙光
- **构图特征**: 广角废墟全景、人物渺小对比巨大废弃建筑、低饱和度中的单点高光

## 悬疑推理 Mystery/Thriller

- **关键词**: dramatic chiaroscuro, noir-inspired shadows, rain-streaked windows, evidence close-ups, tense character confrontations, dimly lit interiors, red string conspiracy boards, clock motifs suggesting urgency, reflections in broken mirrors
- **色彩**: 低调冷色——深青、灰蓝、暗紫，搭配警告红或证据黄作为点缀
- **参考作品**: 端脑、暗河传
- **构图特征**: 分割构图暗示双面性、窥视视角、大面积阴影吞噬画面、聚光灯式局部照明

## 校园青春 Campus/Youth

- **关键词**: school uniforms with personalized details, cherry blossom lined pathways, classroom golden hour light, rooftop hangouts, bicycle rides, shoe lockers, sports festival scenes, summer fireworks, graduation ceremonies, handwritten notes
- **色彩**: 清透明亮——天空蓝、樱花粉、草地绿、夕阳橙、教室里的蜂蜜色光线
- **参考作品**: 你好旧时光、全职高手（校园篇）、龙族
- **构图特征**: 逆光剪影、走廊纵深透视、窗边人物侧脸特写、运动场的广角全景
