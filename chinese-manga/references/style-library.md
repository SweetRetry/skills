# 国漫绘画风格关键词库

## 水墨 Ink Wash

- **Prompt 关键词**: Chinese ink wash painting, sumi-e brush strokes, ink splatter, rice paper texture, varying ink density from deep black to pale grey, dry brush texture, wet-on-wet ink bleeding, calligraphic linework
- **视觉特征**: 墨分五色（焦浓重淡清）、飞白笔触、留白大面积、晕染过渡
- **最佳搭配题材**: 仙侠、武侠、古风
- **排斥项**: photorealistic, 3d render, neon colors, digital noise

## 工笔 Gongbi

- **Prompt 关键词**: gongbi fine brushwork, meticulous detail, delicate ink outlines filled with mineral pigments, silk painting texture, gold leaf accents, layered transparent color washes, precise hair-thin linework
- **视觉特征**: 极致精细的线描勾勒、矿物色层层渲染、工整而非写意、装饰性构图
- **最佳搭配题材**: 古风、仙侠
- **排斥项**: loose brushwork, rough sketch, impressionistic, gritty texture

## 赛璐璐 Cel-shading

- **Prompt 关键词**: cel-shaded illustration, flat color blocks with hard shadow edges, clean digital linework, anime-inspired shading, distinct light and shadow separation, bold outlines, vivid saturated colors
- **视觉特征**: 色块分明、阴影边缘锐利、线条干净均匀、动画感强烈
- **最佳搭配题材**: 热血战斗、都市奇幻、校园青春
- **排斥项**: soft gradient, painterly, watercolor bleeding, textured brush

## 厚涂 Impasto Digital Painting

- **Prompt 关键词**: thick digital painting, visible brush strokes, rich impasto texture, layered oil paint effect, dramatic color blending, heavy pigment buildup, painterly rendering with depth
- **视觉特征**: 笔触可见且有厚度感、色彩浓郁层叠、类油画质感、强调体积与光影
- **最佳搭配题材**: 仙侠、武侠、热血战斗、末日废土
- **排斥项**: flat color, clean linework, vector art, minimal shading

## 水彩 Watercolor

- **Prompt 关键词**: watercolor illustration, transparent color washes, soft wet-on-wet bleeding, visible paper grain texture, delicate pigment granulation, white of paper as highlights, organic color pooling at edges
- **视觉特征**: 颜色透明、水渍自然晕开、纸张纹理可见、边缘柔和有机
- **最佳搭配题材**: 古风、都市言情、校园青春、仙侠
- **排斥项**: sharp digital lines, neon glow, heavy contrast, metallic texture

## 半写实 Semi-Realistic

- **Prompt 关键词**: semi-realistic digital painting, detailed facial features with stylized proportions, realistic skin texture and subsurface scattering, accurate fabric draping, cinematic lighting, photographic depth of field with illustrated elements
- **视觉特征**: 人物比例接近真实但五官略有美化、皮肤有真实质感、衣物褶皱物理正确、电影级打光
- **最佳搭配题材**: 都市言情、都市奇幻、悬疑推理、武侠
- **排斥项**: chibi, flat color, cartoon proportions, sketch lines

## 扁平插画 Flat Illustration

- **Prompt 关键词**: flat vector illustration, geometric shapes, limited color palette, clean edges without gradients, graphic design aesthetic, minimal shadows using solid color overlays, bold silhouettes
- **视觉特征**: 无渐变纯色块、几何化造型、强调图形设计感、极简阴影
- **最佳搭配题材**: 都市言情、校园青春
- **排斥项**: realistic texture, detailed shading, painterly, gritty, noise
